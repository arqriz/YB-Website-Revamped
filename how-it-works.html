What You Can Drop Off

We Accept All Textiles

Clean or worn, new or old — if it's fabric, we'll take care of it. No sorting required on your end.

👕

Clothing

T-shirts, pants, dresses, jackets, underwear, socks — all sizes, all conditions. Kids, adults, workwear, sportswear.

👟

Shoes & Accessories

Sneakers, heels, sandals, boots, bags, belts, scarves, hats, and sunglasses. Pairs preferred but singles accepted.

🛏

Linens & Home Textiles

Bedsheets, towels, curtains, cushion covers, tablecloths, soft toys. Anything fabric that you no longer need.

What we can't accept

Wet or heavily soiled items, furniture, hard plastics, electronics, or hazardous materials. When in doubt, if it's fabric — we've got it covered.

Behind the Scenes

What Happens to Your Donation

Once it's in the bin, here's the transparent journey every item goes through.

Collected

Our team empties bins on a regular schedule — usually every few days.

Sorted

Items are manually sorted by condition — wearable, reusable, or recyclable raw material.

Redistributed

Wearable items go to charities, welfare homes, and community organisations.

Recycled

Non-wearable items are shredded into fibres for industrial reuse — insulation, padding, and new textiles.
