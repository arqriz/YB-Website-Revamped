Our Identity

Who Are the Yellow Bin?

Yellow Bin is Malaysia's leading textile recycling initiative. We place bright yellow collection bins across the country — in malls, schools, condominiums, mosques, and community spaces — making it effortless for everyone to recycle their unwanted clothing and textiles.

Founded with the conviction that Malaysia can do better when it comes to textile waste, we've grown from a small idea into a nationwide network of over 500 collection points. Every bin we place is a promise to the community: your old clothes will never end up in landfill.

We work with charities, recycling partners, and upcyclers to give every textile a second life — whether that means redistribution to those in need, upcycling into new products, or responsible fibre recycling.

Our Purpose

Why Yellow Bin?

Malaysia generates hundreds of thousands of tonnes of textile waste each year. Most of it ends up in landfills — releasing harmful gases and squandering resources that could be recovered and reused.

We chose yellow because it's impossible to miss — just like the problem of textile waste. Our bins are bright, our purpose is clear, and our commitment to Malaysia's sustainability future is absolute.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Everything we do is guided by a simple belief: every piece of fabric deserves a second chance.

Make Recycling Effortless

A Malaysia where no usable textile ever ends up in a landfill — where circular fashion is the norm, not the exception, and communities thrive through shared resources.

Get Involved

Could You Make a Difference as a Volunteer?

Yellow Bin is powered by a passionate community of volunteers who believe in our mission. Volunteers help with bin management, community awareness drives, sorting & distribution, and event organisation.

Whether you have one hour or one day a week, every contribution matters. Join us and be part of something bigger than yourself.

Q What does a volunteer do?

Help manage bin locations, assist at community drives, spread awareness at schools and events, or support our sorting team.

Q How much time is required?

As little or as much as you can give. We have flexible one-off events as well as ongoing roles for regular volunteers.

Q Who can volunteer?

Anyone! Individuals, students, companies, and organisations are all welcome. No experience needed — just a willingness to help.

Accepted Items

What Can We Recycle?

Yellow Bin accepts a wide range of items — not just clothing. Here's everything you can drop off at any Yellow Bin near you.

Paper

  • · Books & magazines
  • · Newspapers
  • · Cardboard & boxes
  • · Printed paper
MAIN

Clothing & Textiles

  • · All clothing & apparel
  • · Shoes & bags
  • · Bed linen & towels
  • · Curtains & cushions

Household Items

  • · Soft toys & plush
  • · Belts & accessories
  • · Hats & scarves
  • · Fabric-based items

Uniforms & Workwear

  • · School uniforms
  • · Corporate workwear
  • · Sports & team kit
  • · PPE & protective gear

Items don't need to be clean or in perfect condition — we accept worn, torn, and faded textiles. The only things we cannot accept are wet, mouldy, or heavily soiled items.
