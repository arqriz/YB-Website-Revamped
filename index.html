Frequently Asked Questions

Soalan Lazim

常见问题解答

Everything you need to know about recycling your textiles with Yellow Bin.

Yellow Bin is one of Malaysia's pioneer in recycling and textile social enterprise. We place convenient collection bins across Johor Bahru so you can drop off old clothes, bags, and fabrics anytime for free.

You can donate clothing, textiles, bags, shoes, accessories, uniforms, workwear, and household fabrics like curtains and towels. Items should be clean and dry.

We have 15+ bin locations across Johor Bahru. Use our Find a Bin page to locate the nearest drop-off point.

Yes, dropping off your textiles at a Yellow Bin is completely free. Simply bring your items to the nearest bin and drop them in, no registration or appointment needed.

Donated or recycled textiles will be sorted and redistributed to those in need, repurposed for industrial use or recycled into new materials, keeping them out of landfill and reducing textile waste in Malaysia.
