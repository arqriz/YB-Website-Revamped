Why Choose Us

A Smarter Way to Give Clothes a Second Chance

We make textile recycling effortless for individuals and responsible for the planet. No guessing, no guilt, just a simple, transparent process you can trust.

Zero Landfill Commitment
Every piece dropped off is tracked and diverted from landfill. We report transparently on what happens to your donations.
Community First
Wearable clothes are redistributed to local charities and community groups first, helping people and the planet at once.
Convenient Collection
Bins are placed in convenient, high-footfall locations. Open 24/7 with no appointment, no forms, no fuss.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about recycling your textiles with Yellow Bin.

Yellow Bin is Malaysia's leading textile recycling social enterprise. We place convenient collection bins across Johor, Selangor, Kuala Lumpur, and Melaka so you can drop off old clothes, bags, and fabrics anytime, for free.

You can donate clothing, textiles, bags, shoes, accessories, uniforms, workwear, and household fabrics like curtains and towels. Items should be clean and dry.

We have 500+ bin locations across Johor, Selangor, Kuala Lumpur, and Melaka. Use our Find a Bin page to locate the nearest drop-off point.

Yes, dropping off your textiles at a Yellow Bin is completely free. Simply bring your items to the nearest bin and drop them in, no registration or appointment needed.

Donated textiles are sorted and either redistributed to those in need, repurposed for industrial use, or recycled into new materials, keeping them out of landfill and reducing textile waste in Malaysia.
